The City of Gates

Myst, known to some as The City of Gates, is a place that sequesters itself among the realms of the multiverse, keeping intentionally hidden to prevent accidental arrival within its walls. Legends speak of a wizard whose magical prowess was without equal who created the realm long, long ago, who was known only as the Wizard of Myst. Here, you will find expanded lore for this realm.
